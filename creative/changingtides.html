Changing Tides - The Ways the Waves Take Us

Book Overview

Changing Tides is an inspiring journey through personal growth and transformation, woven together with reflections on life’s ebbs and flows. Drawing from the power of the ocean as a metaphor, the book explores themes of resilience, adaptability, and self-discovery. It offers readers insight into navigating challenges, embracing change, and finding purpose amidst life’s inevitable shifts.

Purpose and Audience

Targeted at readers interested in personal development and introspection, Changing Tides combines memoir elements with universal lessons. It invites readers to dive deep into their own experiences, encouraging them to ride the waves of life with courage and an open heart. The book also fosters community by linking to Search for the Good, where readers can connect with like-minded individuals and take positive action.

Social Impact

In partnership with the Stroke Foundation, a portion of the book’s revenue is dedicated to supporting stroke survivors, funding therapy services, and promoting recovery journeys. Changing Tides aims to inspire readers while also contributing to positive change beyond its pages.

Summary:

Changing Tides is a story of triumph, as a young 18-year-old, goes on an unimaginable journey to chase his dream of surfing in the Gold Coast. Surviving a stroke, overcoming obstacles, and the unpredictable and ever-changing waves that life brings him.

Changing Tides is also a reflection on life, a metaphor for change and an homage to the main character's love for surfing. Each chapter takes the reader on a roller coaster ride of emotion and shows us all the ways the waves of life can take us, while sharing key lessons learned throughout the journey. Using personal surfing analogies, and references to the ocean, the author illustrates key themes like change, overcoming challenges, growth, letting go, self-love, freedom, and more throughout the book.

However, this unconventional story has a twist ending… At 26-years of age, his story is not yet over. The last chapter, unfinished, outlines what the main character dreams of creating in what he calls the “deep blue”, a metaphor for the ocean of opportunity in the world. He shares the ripples he’d like to leave to make the world a better place and outlines how readers can join him in making this happen.

Chapters:

Chapter 01: Change - watching the waves
Set on Bondi Beach in Sydney, the main character watches the sunrise as he reflects on the inevitable changes that come with life. He looks back at the past 8 years of his life that have taken him to this moment, the 8 years since he had his stroke.

Chapter 02: Challenge - learning to surf
Set in Long Beach in Tofino, the main character re-experiences a memory of learning how to surf for the first time. It reminds him of the stark challenges he'd once overcome, all of the times he'd fallen that had taught him to get back up.

Chapter 03: Growth - rising above the waves
Set on a beach in San Diego, the main character re-experiences a memory of battling the waves. He reflects on times where he rose above waves that were no longer for him, times he'd chosen growth over ego.

Chapter 04: Letting Go - swimming through waves
Set on a surf trip in Santa Cruz, the main character re-experiences a memory in the water where he had to choose to catch a wave or to let it go. He reflects on learning to accept harsh truths and finding peace through letting go.

Chapter 05: Self-love - one with the waves
Set in Snapper Rocks in the Gold Coast, the main character catches a perfect wave. He reflects on moments where he learned to make peace with his mind.

Chapter 06: Freedom - open water
Set on a ship out at sea, the main character looks out at a massive ocean of opportunity ahead of him. He boards his plane to Australia, excited for the adventures and experiences that await.

Chapter 07: Redirection - taken by the tides
Set in Melbourne, Australia in his new apartment, his future plans disintegrate before his eyes. All his life plans shift abruptly, as his life path gets re-directed.

Chapter 08: Life - going with the flow
Set in Brisbane at the start of his backpacking trip, the main character watches the river flow under the bridge. He starts a journey of just living life spontaneously and going where the water takes him.

Chapter 09: Home - back to shore
Set in his room in his childhood home, the main character leans back on his black leather chair and reflects on where he is now in his life. Grounded in humility, he gains a deeper appreciation for his roots and his responsibility here on Earth.

Chapter 10: Inspiration - the moon and the tide
Set in Main Beach in Agnes Waters during the dark of night, the main character looks out at a meteor shower as the moon and tide remind him of the things he looks up to. He reflects on his role models and values that have inspired him and led him to the person he is today.

Chapter 11: Unfinished - there will always be more waves
Set on White Haven Beach, the main character reflects on the future. He shares his thoughts, optimism, lessons he's learned, ideas, and opportunities to make the world a better place.
