Let Go - Track 9 from Everything Makes Music

Track Overview

Track 9: Let Go is a heartfelt song from my ongoing album project, Everything Makes Music, which explores how life’s experiences—no matter how painful or challenging—can be transformed into something beautiful. The album revolves around the idea that while life brings a variety of sounds, it’s up to us to compose those sounds into music. Let Go reflects the moment of release, when we find peace and clarity after struggles, learning to embrace life as it is. I also created cover art for this track to visually capture its essence.

Theme and Lyrics

The song captures the bittersweet yet uplifting process of letting go, finding one’s place, and moving forward. With imagery of seeds floating and finding their home, Let Go symbolizes growth, acceptance, and the beauty of transformation. The lyrics blend themes of self-discovery, resilience, and hope, reminding listeners that although life has its ups and downs, it’s possible to create harmony from the chaos.

Musical Style

Let Go combines soft, acoustic elements with an evocative melody, encouraging reflection and serenity. The song’s chorus and rhythm evoke a sense of freedom and release, reinforcing the theme of embracing life’s journey and finding music in every experience.
